Headguard Halo

Good Health and Wellbeing

the Headguard Halo is a headband made out of lightweight breathable material which is used to detect an incidents that have occured on the head The product I have made contains, 2 force Sensors and a adxl345 accelerometer they both are used for measuring the impact of the damage done to the head. I used a ESP32 instead of a arduino because it helps to send wireless signals and has bluetooth , LED Indicator and Buzzer Alarm which are used to alert the player about the injury, in the future scope we will have Real-Time Data Transmission which Sends alerts to coaches and medical staff instantly. For the power source we have a 3.7 volt battery with a type c outlet because they are more universally available.
